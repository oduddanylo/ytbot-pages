Privacy Policy – ytbot

Last updated: 12 August 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how ytbot (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you connect TikTok, we receive your basic profile information as permitted by TikTok’s API.
  • Content Data: Videos, captions, and metadata you choose to publish.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and usage logs for security and analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your TikTok account via OAuth.
  • To process and publish your videos to TikTok.
  • To improve, secure, and maintain our Service.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with:

  • TikTok, as necessary to publish your videos.
  • Service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics) under confidentiality agreements.
  • Authorities, if required by law.

4. Data Retention

We store your information only as long as necessary to provide the Service, or as required by law.

5. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

6. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy at any time. The updated version will be posted on our website.
